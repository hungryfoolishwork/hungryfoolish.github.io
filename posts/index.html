Decaffeinated

Friday, March 25, 2022

Temptation is strong this morning. I’m rounding out week four of a months long abstinence from caffeine. The tricks my mind is playing are probably no different than an addict of any other substance. Why wait another week, it’s basically been a month? This is a silly game? What’s the difference between today and next Friday? I concede this is somewhat arbitrary but in the end I’ll be somewhat proud of myself.

Getting Started

Wednesday, February 23, 2022

Plywood is wonderful. You can trust with almost certainty it’s flat and square which jumpstarts your adventures (baltic birch is your friend). There are gobs of YouTubers making cool things with plywood. I could not stop watching Chris Salomone build all kinds of furniture, have no idea how I found him but he does a great job inspiring by way of talking through the process. Cristiana at Get Hands Dirty has some great builds as well that are almost meditative to sit through.
