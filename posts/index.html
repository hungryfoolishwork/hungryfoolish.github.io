Getting Started

Wednesday, February 23, 2022

Plywood is wonderful. You can trust with almost certainty it’s flat and square which allows you to jumpstart your adventures. Baltic birch is your friend. There are gobs of YouTubers making cool things with plywood. I could not stop watching Chris Salomone build all kinds of furniture, have no idea how I found him but he does a great job filming and talking through the process. Cristiana at Get Hands Dirty has some great builds that are almost meditative to sit through.
