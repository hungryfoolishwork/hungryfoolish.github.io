Tools

Tools are an extension of our hands and body. Only the very best earn the right to be in my possession in an attempt to reduce waste and ensure what I’m working with is reliable and enabling. Brands get in the way of assessing a good tool so I’m not recommending any, instead I’m just revealing what I’ve chosen through a lot of research and help from books like The Anarchist’s Toolchest which go into great detail to explain what a good tool is without being a hawker of brand names.

This list is a work in progress.

Handplanes

Much time is spent flattening, squaring up and smoothing so these tools are essential. The Jack Plane will become a dear friend so chose wisely. These are roughly listed in order of importance:

  • No. 5 jack plane
  • No. 7 jointer plane
  • No. 4 smoothing plane
  • No. 60 block plane
  • Large & small router planes
  • Shoulder plane
  • Spokeshave
